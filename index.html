360 Walkthrough - Advanced Navigation Research Simulator (ANRS)

ANRS is the only dedicated navigation research simulator, which is made up of Full Mission Ship-handling Simulator (FMSS) and Vessel Traffic Information System (VTIS), in South East Asia. It allows CEMS to work closely with Singapore Maritime Academy to enhance the training assessment and delivery for traditional seafarers for manned vessels.

The ANRS enables CEMS and our partners to research into human factors and build AI assessment systems which could be deployed at other training institutions.

Leveraging on ANRS, CEMS is in close partnership with PSA Marine to embrace technologies to enhance training and assessment for our Harbour Pilots. Both parties shared a common vision - The collaboration on AI assessment of navigation and ship handling competency for our pilots is a great move to study the human elements that is critical to maritime safety.
